Chair Yoga
  1. Sit with your back straight and make yourself as tall as possible, lower your shoulders and let your arms hang by your sides Sweep up with your arms and extend them as you breathe in, breathe out and sweep the arms.
  2. Swep your arms up over your head, inhale, lean back as far as you can
  3. Exhale at the same time as you bend down over yours legs with hands down to the floor. Relax take 3 breaths
  4. Come up In a sitting position with a straight back, lowered shoulders And lift your left leg straight out the hold for about 5 seconds
  5. Bend the leg in towards the body, grab around the knee and aim to touch the knee with your forehead.

Then start over from 1 again but this time you should lift the right leg.
If you want you can do this several turns but try to do the same amount on both sides
Finish by sitting upright.

Standing Yoga
  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart with a straight back and do you as long as possible. Raise your left arm straight diagonally to the right and raise your right arm Bend so that the left arm rests in the crease of the arm and pull the left arm towards the body with the right And at the same time turn your head to the left. Take 3 deep calm breaths here.
    Do the same with the opposite arm.
  2. Stand hip-width apart behind your chair and take a light support with your hands on the back support. Stretch your back. Bend your legs and go down as far as you feel comfortable while exhaling, breathe in and stretch your legs and come up on your toes. Repeat at least 3 times
  3. Stand on the right side of the chair with a straight back with your feet wide apart, lean to the left side with a straight back and stretch your right arm straight over your head until you can reach the seat of the chair with left hand while exhaling.
    Breathe in and come up to an upright position with your arms at your side.
    Repeat at least 3 times, repeat on the other side.
  4. Stand with your feet hip-width apart with a straight back and do you as long as possible. Lower your shoulders and raise both arms over your head and grasp with right hand around the left wrist and pull it slightly to the right over the head while tilting the body to the right.
    Take three calm deep breaths here and then come up straight and come down with your arms at your sides. Repeat with the opposite side.

If you want you can do this several turns.
Finish by standing upright.
