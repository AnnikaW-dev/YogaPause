About YogaPause

This site is for those of you who sit still in front of the computer during the day, studying for a job or playing. Because you can easily get pain in your shoulders and back. In order to prevent inflammation and chronic pain, it is good to move a little from time to time to get the blood flowing going.
You can choose between Chair Yoga or Standing Yoga depending on your mood at the moment. Here can you also choose a guided meditation or breathing exercise to lower your stress level if you are feeling stressed.
Taking a break from what you're doing can re-energize and make you see new solutions.
